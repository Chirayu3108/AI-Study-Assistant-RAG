Data Science Overview
    Data science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistical analysis, machine learning, and domain expertise to extract insights and knowledge from data. It involves the collection, processing, and analysis of large volumes of data to uncover patterns, trends, and relationships that can inform decision-making and drive business value.
    The data science process typically includes several key steps, such as data collection, data cleaning and
    preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, model building, and model evaluation. Data scientists use a variety of tools and techniques to perform these tasks, including programming languages like Python and R, as well as specialized libraries and frameworks for data manipulation, visualization, and machine learning.
    Data science has applications across various industries, including finance, healthcare, marketing, and social media

    key Applications of Data Science:
    1. Predictive Analytics: Data science can be used to build predictive models that forecast
    future outcomes based on historical data.
    2. Customer Segmentation: Data science can help businesses segment their customers based on
    demographics, behavior, and preferences, allowing for targeted marketing and personalized experiences.
    3. Fraud Detection: Data science can be used to identify fraudulent activities and transactions by
    analyzing patterns and anomalies in the data.
